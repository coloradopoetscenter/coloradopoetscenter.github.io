Critical Commentary

“The humanity of this rare combination—the healing art and the art of poetry—is here in abundance.”
          Reg Saner

“George Young cuts to the truth of experience and transforms it. These are poems in which the particular incident glows, warming and nourishing the reader.”
          Jack Coulehan

“A fine poet and excellent storyteller. His poems are vivid celebrations of seeing and hearing; of the art, music, and the healing word; of nature and medicine—rendered close up and compelling.”
          John Stone
