Biography

George Young, born in Mesa, Arizona, received degrees from Arizona State University and the University of Southern California Medical School. He practiced at the Boulder Medical Center for 32 years, specializing in Internal Medicine and Rheumatology, before retiring in Boulder. He now enjoys his family )(wife Peg, a retired Hospice Nurse; two grow children, Greg and Shelby; and one grandchild, on the way), walking his golden retriever, working crossword puzzles and looking at birds through his binoculars.

George's website is www.drgeorgeyoung.com
