Art has published in journals, newspapers and literary magazines all over the country, most recently Forum for Applied Research and Public Policy (Tennessee), Haight-Ashbury Literary Journal (San Francisco), Zephyr (Utah), Central Avenue (New Mexico), Word,Metaphor, Mountain Gazette, Art’s Perspective, Silverton Mountain Journal and Mad Blood (Colorado). He has given hundreds of poetry readings in Colorado and around the country including EAR, Vail’s Festival of Words (2003, 2004), North Fork Valley Earth Day (Paonia), Poets & Politics Roundtable (Crested Butte), the “Sparrows” festival for many years, and he maintains a constant presence at the Gourd Circle Reading Series (Telluride).
