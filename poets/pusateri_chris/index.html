Biography

Chris Pusateri was born in Ohio and has lived in London, Mexico City and Kingston, Jamaica. He is the author of several books and chapbooks of poetry, most recently Molecularity (Dusie, 2011) and Anon (BlazeVOX, 2008).  His poetry and critical prose has appeared in American Letters & Commentary, Boston Review, Chicago Review, Jacket and Verse, among others. A librarian by profession, he resides in Denver.  His website is here.
