Biography

Cameron Keller Scott was born in Denver, CO in 1977 and graduated from Whitman College in Walla Walla, WA.  He received a Master of Fine Arts, Poetry, from the University of Arizona in 2004 where he taught a creative writing workshop at Voices 110 Degrees, a writing program for at risk youth in Tucson, AZ for two years before taking a road trip in search of his roots and trout.  He currently lives and works in the Roaring Fork Valley.
