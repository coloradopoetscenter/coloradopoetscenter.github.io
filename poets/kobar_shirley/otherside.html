Poems: Haibun | On the Other Side | How Many Strokes of Cloth

On the Other Side

of mountain
past   eternal
views of peaks
leaning on  peaks 
snow
courses   rivers  
horizons     flash
slip into  seas
new leaves
of your eyes
birthing spring
  trees dream
peach summers
at  winters end
