How Many Strokes of Cloth

does it take
to remove silver plate from a copper  tin frame?
I rub the cloth over and    over    spreading
silver   on whimsy of bamboo and flower

remove silver plate from a copper tin frame.
The maple syrup pitcher iridescent green
silver  on whimsy of bamboo and flower
fingers cramp clutching fabric.

The maple syrup pitcher iridescent green
Great-great-grandmother Mary Kane McCoy
whose fingers cramp clutching fabric
silver plated pitcher in a covered wagon.

Great-great-grandmother Mary Kane McCoy
traveling from Pennsylvania to Iowa
silver plated pitcher in a covered wagon
with husband, Thomas, and their three children.

Traveling from Pennsylvania to Iowa
imagination limited by youth  did not dream
Thomas and Mary, Michael, and Ella  -
dust in their lungs   water    food.

Imagination limited by youth   did not dream
burnt bread      bruises from rutted jolts
dust in their lungs   water   food
Ella coughing into her apron.

Burnt bread      bruises from rutted jolts
husband fearing their leaving
Ella  coughing into her apron.
Mother  father brother sisters    beyond the overland trail.

Husband sweating their leaving
maple syrup pitcher iridescent green.
Mother  father brother sisters    beyond the overland trail
How many           does it take?

(Red Ochre Journal)
