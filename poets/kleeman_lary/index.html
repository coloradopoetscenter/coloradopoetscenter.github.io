Biography

Lary Kleeman was born and raised in Denver. Kleeman teaches high school English. From 1992-94, Kleeman taught conversational English in the Peace Corps in Estonia. A graduate of the MFA program at the University of Montana, Kleeman is a recipient of the Colorado Council on the Arts Poetry Fellowship (2002).

His 2010 chapbook, Negotiating a Lower Angle, is available at http://www.blurb.com/bookstore/detail/2066550
