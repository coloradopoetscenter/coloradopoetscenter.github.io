Readings / Workshop Availability

SETH has been performing and conducting workshops for more than 20 years. He is versatile in working with all age groups and can offer a variety of programs including customizing his presentation to a specific audience or occasion.

SETH enjoys performing both solo and with an ensemble. He performs regularly with musicians and frequently incorporates fellow poets, actors, singers, dancers, etc. into his presentations. He has traveled Colorado extensively giving featured readings, appearing at poetry festivals and other literary events. He also has extensive experience producing and/or coordinate poetry events, variety shows and professional productions. In 2001 he authored the hit musical revue “Suite Moses.”

SETH currently works with Colorado Humanities, Lighthouse Writers and Summer Scholars (among others) teaching poetry, creative writing, storytelling and acting – primarily to elementary and middle school students. In the past, he has coordinated/directed countless shows and school assembly programs, his students often writing and performing their own material.
