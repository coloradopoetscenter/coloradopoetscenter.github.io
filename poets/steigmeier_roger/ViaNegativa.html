Via Negativa

(Reflection On A Church In Darkness)

In stillness and shadow
lives the light.

Do not run from the night

darkness is radiant

the soul’s delight.

If you can see in the dark
there is nothing to fear.

If you can see

steeple and cross
in silhouette

signal not

past malice
or scorched flesh

If you can see

grace glows and grows
in the depth of darkness.
