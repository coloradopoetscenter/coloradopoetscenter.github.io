Ghost Orchid

(The ghost orchid does not grow in soil. It isa species of orchid that grows on trees. The ghost orchidblends so well with the tree bark that the striking white flower appears to be suspended in midair.  While the flower is in bloom, the giant sphinx moth pollinates it, thus insuring its survival.)

Even in the dim lunar glow
of a half moon

the seraphim is radiant

a divine spectral presence
suspended in a summer night.

Only in disenchanted daylight
are its roots visible--

green cords wrapped around
a tree.

surviving only on what
its air born lover

brings it.

Strange angel, then
that must wait

for penetration

from a winged creature.
that was once

an earthbound crawler.

Anxious angel, too
that longs for night

so that it can dream again
of being free

of roots and tree

and its moth consort

dream of taking flight
into the firmament

into the charmed embrace
of the stars

and the cool caress

of moonbeams.
