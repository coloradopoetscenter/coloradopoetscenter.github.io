Bibliography

Prayer to a Purple God (Mellen Poetry Press, 1996; reissued in hardback in 2004)

Studer’s poems have appeared in many literary journals including Bloomsbury Review, Sing Heavenly Muse!, Cream City Review, Minnesota Review, Carquinez Poetry Review, North Dakota Quarterly and her work has appeared in anthologies, including We Speak For Peace (Knowledge, Ideas, and Trends, 1993); Prairie Smoke: The Pueblo Poetry Project 1979-1989 (Univ. of Southern Colorado Press, 1990) and Wingbone: Poetry from Colorado (Sudden Jungle Press, 1987) and Toward Solomon’s Mountain: The Experience of Disability in Poetry (Temple University Press, 1986).

Studer has also published a variety of creative non-fiction essays, reviews, short stories, and translations.
