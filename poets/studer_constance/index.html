Biography

Constance Studer earned a diploma from the Toledo Hospital School of Nursing and worked as a registered nurse in Intensive Care/Coronary Care and as a hospital supervisor. She earned a B. A. in English Literature form Illinois College, Jacksonville, Illinois, and a M. A. in Creative Writing from the University of Colorado, Boulder. Beside working as a registered nurse, she taught creative writing in the Continuing Education Department of the University of Colorado.
