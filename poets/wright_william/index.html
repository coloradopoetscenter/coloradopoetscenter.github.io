Biography

William Winfield Wright is a Fulbright Scholar and Professor of English at Colorado Mesa University in Grand Junction. He has published in The Beloit Poetry Journal, Field, Weber Studies, Third Coast, and many other places. His chapbook Cosmonauts is available from Wormwood Chapbooks of San Dimas, California. A second chapbook, Bird Feet, will be available in the Fall of 2012.
