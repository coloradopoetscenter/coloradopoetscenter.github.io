All in an Effort to Not Think About

Rise,
stretch
bathe, dry,

crack, whisk,
sizzle, fry,

then drive, dash, do

cross the list
through and through

hike, teach,
call, reach

meet
eat

brush
rush

slide
hide

cry
lie.
