Readings / Workshop Availability

Uche Ogbuji is a heavily experienced speaker and facilitator, having made appearances for years on high technology topics as well as poetry. He is available for readings and a variety of workshops, including at all levels of education. Examples of workshops he has facilitated include "Poetry from the Heart's Far-flung Places," "African Griot Techniques, Abroad," and "Muse of Memory."
