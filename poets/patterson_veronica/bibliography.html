Bibliography

How to Make a Terrarium, Cleveland State University (1987).

This Is the Strange Part, Pudding House Publications, 2002, chapbook of prose poems.

The Bones Remember: A Dialogue, with photographer Ronda Stone (Stone Graphics Press), poetry and photography.

“Comfort Me with Apples,” Georgia Review, Spring 1997, selected as a Notable Essay.
“Feast,” Pilgrimage 2003.
“My Griefs to Sing: Creative Writing and Loss,” forthcoming in Teachers & Writers Collaborative.
