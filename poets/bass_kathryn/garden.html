Poems: A Song for Seeds | Firefly | Garden of Skirts | Garden of Dreams

Garden of Skirts 

strange wind

from beneath

blossom

 

the bright seeds

the tentacles

of the things

 

elastic

recoil and float

 

fingers

 

searching

flat swings

 

(from Bright Seeds, forthcoming from Finishing Line Press)
