His first book of poetry, Span (Rhizome Publishing, 2011, 2012) was published
as an eBook before being published in paperback. His other publishing credits
include poems published in the books Peaceful Poetry to Love Your Societal 
Consciousness (Ann McCall 2001) and Baby Blessings (Harmony Books, 2002) as well
as poems published in in Poetry Motel, Junction, Mountain Alchemy and articles in
the Pueblo P.U.L.P. and the Mountain Park News.
