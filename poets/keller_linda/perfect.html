Perfect Afternoon

Above

a curved roof of woven branches

triangles of light

enter

draw a patchwork quilt

below

 

Tucked under

the comfort of

shadows weight

slivers of crystal blue

peek in

 

Time stops

 

The deep perfume

of summer bliss

inhaled

 

Linda Keller

From Comet Dreams, Tindari Press, 2004
