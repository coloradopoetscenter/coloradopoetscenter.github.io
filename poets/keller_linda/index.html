Biography

Linda Keller is the author of six books of poetry. Her latest volume, Take It and Go On was released in June, 2009. Her previous book, Comet Dreams, was listed as a local bestseller after her reading at the Tattered Cover Book Store in March, 2005.  

After working as a classroom Language Arts teacher for nine years, she has returned to conducting creative writing workshops, focusing on her own writing, and tutoring students.

For more information, visit her website at http://www.ljkeller.com
