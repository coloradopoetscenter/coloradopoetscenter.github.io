Lost Man Trail

Bright red stalks
of fading king's crown
bow to the earth,
supplicants of
newly chilled air.
A weaving at my feet
of gray rock, white pebbles,
green grass and red braids.
Independence Lake mirrors
the sky in the distance,
my eye, a camera,
storing the colors.

(Mountain Gazette, August, 2006)
