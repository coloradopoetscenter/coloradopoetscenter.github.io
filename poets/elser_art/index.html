Bioraphy

Art Elser retired after 20 years as an Air Force pilot. During that time, he flew combat at a forward air controller in Vietnam in 1967-68 and retired in 1979. He worked 30 years as a technical writer for various computer, software, and communications firms. He has a PhD in English and was a professor of English at the USAF Academy, and an adjunct professor at Chapman University, University of Colorado, Colorado Springs and Denver, Phoenix University, American University, and New Mexico Tech.
