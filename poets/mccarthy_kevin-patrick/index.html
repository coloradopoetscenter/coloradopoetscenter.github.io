Kevin Patrick McCarthy

KPMc is a Colorado poet, dramatist, essayist, and geologist. His early poems were well-received, but he didn't return to poetry until the recent death of a friend. Since 2012, 17 of his poems have appeared in various journals, including NEAT, COMMON GROUND REVIEW, and WRITTEN RIVER. “Enough Sky” was commended (top 11 out of more than 13,000 entries) in The Poetry Society's 2014 National Competition (UK) (http://poetrysociety.org.uk/poems/enough-sky/).
