Critical Commentary:

"Laura Weaver’s work is nothing less than an “Axis Mundi” for the spirit. Her negative capability is passionate and leads us from bear, elk, beggar child, lightning, and an Alzheimer’s Unit to an entropic world salvaged in grace.  Her poems are brilliant, compassionate, regenerative, expansive, and memorable."
-Mark Irwin, White City and Bright Hunger
