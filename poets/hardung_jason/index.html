Biography

Jason Hardung's work has appeared in a bunch of journals and magazines including:  3AM, Monkey Bicycle, Evergreen Review, New Wave Vomit, Thought Catalog, Word Riot, Thrasher Magazine and The New York Quarterly.  He has two books of poetry, The Broken and the Damned (Epic Rites Press, 2009) and The Names of Lost Things (Lummox Press, 2012). He has been an editor for Wolverine Farm Publishing and the Front Range Review in Ft. Collins, Colorado.  In 2013 he was voted Poet Laureate of Ft. Collins.
