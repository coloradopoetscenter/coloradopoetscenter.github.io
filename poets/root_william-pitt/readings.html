Readings / Workshop Availability

William Pitt Root enjoys giving readings from his own work, unpublished as well as published poems and stories as well as creative writing workshops, preferably student-work-driven. He works with groups from Kindergarten through Assisted Care, including MFA programs, undergraduate schools, prisons, juvenile detention centers, AA groups, you name it. Poetry like love derives its power from being shared.
