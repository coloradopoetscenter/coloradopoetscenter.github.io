Availability for Readings:

Peter is available for readings individually and as a founding member of the River City Nomads, a performance poetry ensemble. He is also happy to offer workshops, retreats, and school visits. He has led a variety of workshops on prose, poetry, and prose poetry at the High Plains Book Festival, Southwest Writers Conference, and Sparrows Poetry Festival, and Crestone Charter School among other places.
