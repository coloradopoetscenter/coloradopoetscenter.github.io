ivory moon

         the one tonight         rice paper     

the moon turns    lowest part

concealed by sun         ivory        tangerine

so large it seems out of place       

so low it seems fake

the moon skirts the shortest trees      

it will tear       
                 drop        

                               disappear

I know this subterfuge

(from the chapbook double exposure, Plan B Press)
