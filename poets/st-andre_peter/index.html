Biography

Peter Saint-Andre was trained in philosophy and classics at Columbia University, but decided against an academic career and instead has worked as a technologist since the mid-1990s. Although his primary intellectual interests are in philosophy, he has published one collection of poems and translations (Ancient Fire, Monadnock Valley Press, 2013) and is currently working on a cycle of poems about the philosophy of Friedrich Nietzsche./p>
