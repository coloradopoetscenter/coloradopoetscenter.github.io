Readings / Workshop Availability

As  graduate of NYU drama program, I bring exceptional performance and excitement to all of my readings and workshops.  My workshops are very interactive, so everyone can have their own personal experience and aha moments.  Two of my poetry workshops are "Writing Poetry: Letting It Be Easy" and "How to Perform A Poem With Vibrance".  I can also use my acting, writing and improv experience to customize the perfect workshop for your event.  For more speaker and workshop information go to http://inspiredtospeak.com/

If you have an event and would like special entertainment, I am available to perform personalized improv poems for each of your guests.  I can perform at banquets, parties or even as a brain break in between conference sessions,For more improv poet information go to http://www.ImprovPoet.com

I have performed and keynoted at conferences, lead workshops and performed at universities, high schools, festivals and at other venues around the United States and Canada.  Please call or email to check availability. 1-800-26HUMAN (1-800-264-8626) or amym@epoemsaboutlife.com
