If A Dog Stopped Playing

 

If a dog stopped playing
When he became an adult
Everyone would ask, “What is wrong with that poor dog?”
And he would be taken to the vet.
But when we are adults and begin to work
And stop our play
Does anyone take us to the doctor?

Poem from "Poetry for All Those Breathing"
