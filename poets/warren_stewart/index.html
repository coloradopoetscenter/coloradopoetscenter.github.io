Biography

Stewart Warren, author of twelve poetry collections, was born in Tulsa, Oklahoma, in 1950. He hit the road early, engaging in a life of trouble-making and creative expression. As a publishing coach, catalyst and community organizer he helps others to realize their creative visions. Her owns Mercury HeartLink (www.heartlink.com), a small press in New Mexico, and is the founder of the Albuquerque Poet Laureate Program.
