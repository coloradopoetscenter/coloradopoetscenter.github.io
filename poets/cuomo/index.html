Biography

Award winning poet, freelance journalist and inspirational speaker. D. T. Cuomo has a list of credits that include Five by Five, a co-collection of snap-fiction, Song of Salmon, a west coast cookbook, and articles in Kahtou, The Slant, and Mountain Gazette. Recipient of the 2000 Shari Meakin Emerging Writing Award and the Capilano College Scribble for Literacy, she completed her first novel, When You Swim with Dolphins, and is current working on a collection of poems set in Bosnia Herzegovina.
