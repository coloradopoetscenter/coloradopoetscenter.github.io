paradox: success

with grace comes assurance
partial salvation is no salvation at all
there is no such thing as possibility

cast all your cares on him like stones
            they will bounce off like the beer bottle
            i kept throwing at the trunk of an electric tree
NO
they will crush his bones
he will crumple on the ground and petrify into a
stone filling the whole earth and crying out
to the glory of God the Father
                                                amen

the dog
is only as good as his owner
no wonder i hate him with such pity
and they blame me
       they blame him
anyone but themselves

a wise woman once said
            snap out of it
but my bootstraps are tore clean off from
            pulling

what color is your pill reminder?
mine’s purple

here we go

you really were the only one in the world that day
i know
you only thought you were happy dead
just like when you were alive
failure was the only heaven you would ever know
