Blackberries west

My husband eats blackberries
out of our daughter's hand,
she has more palm lines
than could even fit
on my left hand.  We've
moved as far west as we can
for the reason of
eating these plump pockets.
Juice making
of mouth
devour
of anything
even the
freezer
can hold.
