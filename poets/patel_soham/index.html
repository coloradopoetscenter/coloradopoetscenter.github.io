Biography

Soham Patel lives in Manitou Springs.  Her poems and prose have appeared in Copper Nickel, Cranky, SHAMPOO, Stirring, and other places. She teaches writing at the University of Colorado in Colorado Springs, works as a managing editor for Pikes Peak Community College's Almagre Arts Journal, and is a Kundiman fellow.
