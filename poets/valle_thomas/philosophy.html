My Philosophy Of Living

when my mind feels so full, then
another thought comes to mine
the name is daily living, say so, like rain
some one might say, the way advocates live
speaking from the heart/making the dumb talk

founded not by work of monks, or clergy
from the dark platform of night
the attitude of change came upon me
not from poe or keats, but my english
bulldog, who sleeps on a blue chair, in my
living room, his pine cone tail and tooth
that protrudes from the left side of his mouth

these thoughts come and go not daily but
yearly, take pleasure in yourself, take pleasure in being an advocate.

beware the size might amaze you

Thomas George Valle
