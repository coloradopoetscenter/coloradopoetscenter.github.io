Commentary

"Indeed, I was at the Friday performance and very much enjoyed "Wilderness Reflections".  Both the beginning and ending, written by you, captured the essence of Rocky Mountain National Park.   The poetry of Wendell Berry was an added treat.   During the performance, I found myself thinking 'what a fitting tribute to those who had the foresight 100 years ago to set this place aside so that future generations could celebrate.'   May the Rockies sing forever! Thanks to you and the OSEP for this wonderful tribute to the park on the occasion of its 100th anniversary".

Vaughn Baker, Supervisor, Rocky Mountain National Park.
