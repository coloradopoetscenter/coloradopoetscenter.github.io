Readings / Workshop Availability

Marvin Hass has done readings for various community organizations, service clubs and senior citizens groups, in Chaffee County. He is available to those interested in the West and how this “Prairie Poet” gathers material for his poems.

Hass can be reached at home, 31260 Northwoods Circle, Buena Vista, CO 81211, e-mail hassmc@chaffee.net or 719-395-3659.
