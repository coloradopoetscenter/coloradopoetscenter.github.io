I enjoy presenting my poetry to the public and am available for readings and workshops.  I would also happily visit schools to talk about poetry and the poetic process and work with students of all ages who are interested in writing their own poetry.

My work schedule and the time and expense involved in traveling to various locations throughout the state make it necessary to request that anyone interested in scheduling me for an appearance be willing to discuss covering my basic travel expenses and providing a nominal stipend.
