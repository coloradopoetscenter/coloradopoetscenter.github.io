Biography

Daniel Grandbois is the author of the prose poetry/flash fiction collection Unlucky Lucky Days (BOA Editions, 2008), the art novel The Hermaphrodite: An Hallucinated Memoir (Green Integer, 2010), and the prose poetry collection Unlucky Lucky Tales (forthcoming Texas Tech University Press, September 2012). He plays upright bass in Slim Cessna's Auto Club and Tarantella, and software instruments in Munly and the Lupercalians.
