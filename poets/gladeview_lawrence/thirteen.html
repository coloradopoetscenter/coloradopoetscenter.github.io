Thirteen Hundred Miles & Counting

driving westward
an orange
interstate sign
with
black lettering 
came into view

NOTICE
correctional facility
do not 
pick up
hitchhikers 

drawing two 
cowboy killers
from my
shirt pocket,
i turned to
shotgun
& said
if you made it 
this far, i think
you deserve
a free ride.

 

(from Just Ignore The Beer Stains, PigeonBike Press, 2011)
