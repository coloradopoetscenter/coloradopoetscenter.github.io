Reading Availability

I am available at any opportunity to share my poetry at public readings.  I would welcome the opportunity to work with students to engage them in the exercise of writing and help them to be better at the spoken and written word.  I am able at times to make this a collaborative effort by bringing artists and writers and musicians to accompany me.
