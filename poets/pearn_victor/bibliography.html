  • Cryptozoology Poems, Foothills Publishing, CO 2006
  • Postcard from Athens, Kindred Spirit Press, KS 2007
  • Point Guard, Pearn and Associates, CO 2006
  • Light Across the Alley, Kindle Books, CO 2011
  • Dream Season: My Brother Gary and the 1957  Ashland Panthers, Kindle Books, CO 2011
  • Cattails and Sagebrush, Indian Paintbrush Poets, 20132)
