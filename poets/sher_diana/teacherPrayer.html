A Teacher’s Prayer

Everyday
I will tap your arm
Looking for a vein
If I find one
We will share blood
Transfuse ourselves
Transcend ourselves
Transform ourselves
In the sacred temple
Of the classroom
The light will be so bright
Our vision so clear
I will see you
Like you have never been seen
And we will bring forth
Knowledge
From the earth.
