Biography

Native of northwest Denver before boutiques replaced roughage, John Reinhart is a former state fiddle champion, state rhythm guitar champion, one time northeast regional yo-yo champion, and defender of the discarded. He currently lives in Wheat Ridge with his wife, children, and a small menagerie. His poetry has recently been published in 94 Creations, Apeiron Review, Black Heart Magazine, Dirty Chai Magazine, FishFood & LavaJuice, Liquid Imagination, Poetry Nook Magazine, Songs of Eretz, Star*Line, and The Vocabula Review.
