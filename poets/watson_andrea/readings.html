Readings / Workshop Availability

I have taught for many years and have held numerous writing workshops for children and adults. I was Language Arts Instructor and Team Leader at The Ricks Center for Gifted Children at the University of Denver and English Instructor and Chairperson of the Humanities Department at St. Mary's Academy in Denver. I am more than delighted to teach or hold workshops for grades 3-12 and also adults. My area of expertise is creative writing, with an emphasis on helping students to find publication of their work in national student writing magazines and writing contests. I would be happy to work anywhere, with an emphasis on Colorado and New Mexico.
