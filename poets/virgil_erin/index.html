Biography

Erin Virgil is a poet and collage artist living in an RV in northern Colorado (right now, it’s in Golden).  She holds an MFA in creative writing from Naropa University and is currently working on a travelogue about the RV life. If you’d like to read more of her work, please visit her blog: http://emvlovely.wordpress.com.
