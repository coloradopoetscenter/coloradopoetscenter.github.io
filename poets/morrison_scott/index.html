Biography

Scott Morrison was born in Limon, CO and grew up in Meeker, CO. He is a senior at Mount Marty College in Yankton, SD seeking a Bachelor of Arts Degree in English with a Writing Emphasis.

He has five poems published in either Paddlefish or Pasque Petals. He was selected the winner of the Stevens Scholarship Contest by South Dakota State Poetry Society. Scott works as a sports writer at the Yankton Press & Dakotan Daily Newspaper. He also works as an intern at South Dakota Magazine.
