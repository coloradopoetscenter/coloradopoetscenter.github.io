Biography

Kimberly McClintock holds an MFA from the Warren Wilson MFA Program for Writers, and is the recipient of their alumni association’s 2009 Larry Levis Fellowship in poetry. Her essays and poems have appeared or are forthcoming in the following publications: Horse Around New Mexico, Glassworks, Bird’s Thumb, Mountain Gazette, Chatahoochee Review and Wazee Journal. Originally from New Jersey, she lives in Colorado with the writer David Wroblewski.
