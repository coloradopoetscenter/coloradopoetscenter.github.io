Reviews, essays, and critical articles have appeared in numerous publication, including: Boston Review; Jacket; The Poker; Slope; 26; Poetry Project Newsletter; Xantippe; Chase Park; Verse; Sentence: A Journal of Prose Poetics; and in the book Burning Interiors: David Shapiro’s Poetry and Poetics (Fairleigh Dickinson University Press, 2007). Additionally, Gordon writes a quarterly chapbook review column for Rain Taxi: Review of Books.

Poems have appeared in many journals, including: 26: A Journal of Poetry and Poetics, 3rd bed, 580 Split, 88: A Journal of Contemporary American Poetry, Agricultural Reader, Alice Blue Review, Ambit, American Poet, American Letters & Commentary, Antennae, Art New England, Aught, Barn (v.), Bird Dog, Bling Bling, Boog City, Boston Review, Both, Bowl of Stories, Cab/Net, Can We Have Our Ball Back?, Cant, Castagraf, Chase Park, Coconut, Colorado Review, Columbia Poetry Review, Conjunctions (Web), Cul-de Qui (UK), Cypress Magazine, Denver Quarterly, Effing Magazine, EM Literary, Fascicle, Fourteen Hills, Free Verse, Goodfoot, Hambone, Hunger, jubilat, LIT, Lungfull!, Matchbook, Moria, Muse Apprentice Guild, New American Writing, New Review of Literature, Octopus, One Less, Parnassus: Poetry in Review, Parthenon West, Peregrine, Phoebe, The Poker, P-Queue, Shampoo, Shiny, Sleeping Fish, Small Town, Spinning Jenny, Syllogism, The Styles, Upstairs at Duroc (France), Ur Vox, Versal (Holland), Verse, VeRT, Volt, Word/ For Word, Xantippe, Your Black Eye, Zygote (Canada), and others.
