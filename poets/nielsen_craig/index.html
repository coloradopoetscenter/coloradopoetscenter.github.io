Biography

Poet and contemporary folk artist Craig Nielson grew up in Utah and from there traveled the wild places of the American West. He is the host of P3: Poetry, Prose, and Performance, a performance poetry event, host of Radio Poet, a live radio program, and an organizer of the SPARROWS Performance Poetry Festival. He lives in Salida, Colorado with his son, Joshua, and his stray dog, Red.
