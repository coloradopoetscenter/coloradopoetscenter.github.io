Biography

Phil Ray Jack returned to the San Luis Valley of southern Colorado after spending more than twenty years as a "professional part-time English Instructor" in New Mexico and Washington State. Currently, he teaches as an adjunct instructor for Adams State University in Alamosa and spends as much time as possible with his parents and other family members. Besides his poetry, Jack also writes a weekly column for the Valley Courier.
