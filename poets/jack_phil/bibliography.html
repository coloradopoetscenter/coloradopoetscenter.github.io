Bibliography

"The Spirit of the Horse" and Other Works, which includes samples of Phil Ray Jack's poetry and his column.

MAZ (Muse Arts Zine) will include his poems in their May, June and August issues.

Stars in Our Hearts: Revelry (2012) includes his "Another Day."

Two of Jack's poems, "Storm on the Horizon" and "Simply Life," finished among the top 50 in the annual Writer's Digest poetry contest in 2009, and were published in The 4th Annual Writer's Digest" Poetry Awards.

"Eating Fry-Bread with Paula Gunn Allan" was a finalist in Highline Community College's Writing on the Wall poetry contest (2005).
