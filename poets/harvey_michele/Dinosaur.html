Dinosaur Ridge

 On Dinosaur Ridge Dakota Hogback
Sandstone is home to stegosaurus bones
Morrison formation stops in its tracks
Preserved allosaurus bones settle in stone

Fish scales etch in gray shale from a lost sea
A sea whose ripples rest on the freeway
Over tracks of their past birds wander free
These foothills share stories of yesterday

Asteroid collision dusts blocked sun
Plants starved in the darkness of space debris
Just dinosaur trails left in Morrison
Framed by fields of flowers windblown and free

Brontosaurus dinosaur megatracksite
Dinosaur Ridge holds history rock tight

(Evergreen Living Magazine)
