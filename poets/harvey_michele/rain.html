The Meaning of Rain

There’s a diamond in your life,
Unsinkable, infallible, and ever-bright.
Your soul forever grows its light.
We are rainbows in life's prism.
Your soul’s a deep-sea diver.
It swims with every kind of fish.
It reaches with each breath beyond horizons.
When you're thirsty, rain may come to call.
I sometimes need to fall so I remember.
I make my own heaven.
