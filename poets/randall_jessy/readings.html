Readings / Workshop Availability

Jessy Randall loves to give readings, and it is probably because her elementary school had a "Poets in Residence" program that she ever became a writer. She has read at the Richard Hugo House in Seattle, Writers & Books in Rochester, the Happy Ending series in New York, Writers with Drinks in San Francisco, and the Colorado College Visiting Writers series in Colorado Springs. She regularly leads poetry workshops for Poetry West and Author Fest.
