Biography

Aby Kaupang is the author of Absence is such a Transparent House (Tebot Bach, 2011) and Scenic Fences | Houses Innumerable (Scantily Clad Press, 2009).  

Her poems have appeared in VOLT, Verse, Denver Quarterly, The Laurel Review, Parthenon West, Aufgabe, 14 Hills, Interim, Caketrain, lo-ball and others. 

She received her MFA in Creative Writing and is now slogging through her MS in Occupational Therapy. 

Her website is http://www.abykaupang.com
