Bibliography

Jeff started writing poetry over 30 years ago while living in South Africa. A Colorado native, he currently lives in Longmont, Colorado.  A former Chicago stage poet and photographer, Jeff's poems have appeared in numerous print and online magazines, most recently in Blinkzine Arts Magazine, The Argonaut's Boat, Poet Express, The Poetry Super Highway, The Other Voices International Project and Kritya.  Jeff is the founder of The Chicago Poetry Agenda, a former active board member of the Tulsa Arts and Humanities Literary Arts Council and a former member of the Dallas Community of Writers.  He has published 2 books; Fear of Heights (Spahr-Summers Publicaions, 1984) and The Cherry Poems (Lulu/Cherry Publications, 2006).  Jeff is the Publisher and Editor of The Poetry Victims.
