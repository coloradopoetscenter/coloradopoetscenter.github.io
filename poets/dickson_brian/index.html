Biography

Brian has lived mostly in the southwest and west and loves riding his bike around Denver cultivating an awareness of phenomena.He is a firm believer in the power of creativity and how that shapes our lives.  One chapbook, In a Heart’s Rut (2009), was published in a limited edition box set (titled Anything from Anywhere))with Jennifer Moore, Jonathan Pierce and Emily Tipps by High Five Press.  Recent publications include RMNP Poetic Inventory, Pinyon, Quarter After Eight and others.  He is an Associate Professor of Development English at the Community College of Denver.
