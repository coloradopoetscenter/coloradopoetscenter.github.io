Ciletti’s poems have recently appeared in Heartlodge, Pilgrimage, and The Load. His poem "Getting the Newspaper from the Lawn" won the 2004 Literal Latte award. His poem, "Button My Lip" was included in the 2006 Braided Livesprogram in Denver and Taos. His poems have appeared in literary magazines and newspapers, and have won awards, for many years.
