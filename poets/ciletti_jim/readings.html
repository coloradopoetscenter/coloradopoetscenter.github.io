Readings / Workshop Availability

Jim is available for readings, residencies and workshops. He has given readings and workshops throughout the west, including high schools, universities and colleges, prisons, community centers, and libraries. In April of 2004 he served as poet in residence for the Salida, Colorado, April National Poetry month events.; in 2005 he gave the commencement address at the Orme School, Mayer Arizona, and the keynote talk on living with creativity and passion, at the Living Sanctuary, Yucca Valley, California.
