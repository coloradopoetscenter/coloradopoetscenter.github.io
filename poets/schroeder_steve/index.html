Biography

Steven D. Schroeder's first full-length book, Torched Verse Ends, appeared in 2009 from BlazeVOX [books]. His writing is available or forthcoming from Verse, Barrow Street, Beloit Poetry Journal, Pleiades, and Verse Daily. He edits the online poetry journal  Anti- and works as a Certified Professional Résumé Writer.

Steven D. Schroeder now lives in St. Louis.
