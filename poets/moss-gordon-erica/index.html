Biography

Erika Moss Gordon lives in Ridgway, Colorado where she writes poetry, plays in the mountains and loves on her beautiful children, friends & family.  Her first collection of poetry, Of Eyes and Iris, was published by Liquid Light Press in 2013.  Erika’s writing has appeared in Mountain Gazette Magazine, Telluride Watch, Telluride Magazine, Grand Junction Daily Sentinel and 99 Poems for the 99 Percent, a collection of poetry.
