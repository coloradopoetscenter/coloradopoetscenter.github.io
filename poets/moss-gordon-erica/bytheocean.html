By the Ocean

Let’s take a walk,
you and I,
to the end of the road
where we will watch
the blue ocean
in all of its chaos
and dependability.
There is nothing
to say really,
except for what we notice
and what we don’t,
and if there are words
to describe this love
that crashes
and breaks
over and over again
onto the ever shifting sand,
then let us say those, too.
