Biography

Kyle Harvey is the editor of Fruita Pulp. Winner of the Mark Fischer Poetry Prize, his first collection, Hyacinth (Lithic Press, 2013), was a finalist for the Colorado Book Award. His poems have recently appeared or are forthcoming in American Life in Poetry, Electric Cereal, Fat City Review, Heavy Contortionists, Heavy Feather Review, Houseguest, Metatron, New Bile, Ossuary Whispers, Pilgrimage, Reality Hands, SHAMPOO and The Wallace Stevens Journal. His new serial poem July was recently published by Lithic Press.

You can follow him at Twitter.com/kyleharveypoem.
