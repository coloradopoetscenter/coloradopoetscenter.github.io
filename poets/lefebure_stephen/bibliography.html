The Iconoclast, Hellas, Troubadour, Buffalo Bones, Quantum Tao, South Dakota Review, The Neovictorian/Cochlea, Autumnal Equinox, Sulphur River Literary Review, Buffalo Bones and Compass Points, The Ledge, The Adirondack Review, Poetry Depth Quarterly, The Listening Eye, Bibliophilos, The Eagle’s Flight, Compass Points, Romantics Quarterly, Gin Bender, and Evergreen Living.
