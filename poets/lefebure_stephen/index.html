Biography

Stephen Lefebure lives in Evergreen and celebrates the Four Corners States in nature poetry.  He builds treehouses and is devoted to Shiva.  The question of how place matters -- or fails to matter -- is central to his poetry.   In the namedropping department, Lefebure enjoys Martin Heidegger, Mircea Eliade, Christina Rosetti, Wallace Stegner, Holderlin, and Rilke.
