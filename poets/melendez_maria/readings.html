Readings / Workshop Availability

Maria Melendez is available for readings, workshops, manuscript critiques/
editorial consulting (nonfiction and poetry) and customized poetry for special
occasions. She gives readings in a variety of settings, including science centers,
botanic gardens, art galleries and campus venues, and she has served as writer-
in-residence at elementary, middle and high schools with Colorado's WITS
program and California Poets in the Schools. She offers public workshops on a
range of topics, including:

 --Arts & Activism
--Ecopoetics
--Intersections of Identities (environmental, ethnic, artistic & otherwise)
--The Latino Rota: Themes in Contemporary Latino Poetry
--Living the Indie Life: Careers for Writers Outside Academia
--Whens & Whys of Becoming an Editor/Publisher
--The Healing Practice of Poetic Revision
