Readings

I am available for workshops, yes -- I adore doing readings and read whenever I can, and enjoy that with others or solo.  I LOVE to bring poetry to the public.  I have also given workshops in the schools, and am looking to expand on that.  I've only hostessed one slam, but I did a pretty good job, I think.  I also love collaborating with other artists with other art forms (dance, music, etc.).
