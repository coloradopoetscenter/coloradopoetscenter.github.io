Contact/Availability

Susan Palmer is available for readings within a 50 mile radius of Montrose, Colorado, in summers only.  In winter, only in Delta, Montrose, and surrounding areas. 

She has taught a few sessions as a volunteer guest poet at Montrose High School in 2007 and would love to establish a local poets' monthly round-table workshop.
