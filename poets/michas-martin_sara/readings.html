Readings / Workshop Availability

Sara Michas-Martin is available for classroom visits, readings, and workshops. She teaches workshops locally at the Lighthouse in Denver and also through Stanford’s Online Writers Studio and the Rananim Workshop. Please visit her website for upcoming courses and information about one-on-one mentoring and manuscript review guidelines.
