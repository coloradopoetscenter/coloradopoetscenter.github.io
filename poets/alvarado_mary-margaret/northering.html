Northering

I am going
upstairs
by the ship ladder.
I will assemble
a bowl
of pears.
From a standing
dead forest.
From pecan
shells. Small views
of what’s doing
in snow.
The planks
are milled. And me.
Hey season’s folly.
Hey folly. Hey.

(published in No Tell Motel)
