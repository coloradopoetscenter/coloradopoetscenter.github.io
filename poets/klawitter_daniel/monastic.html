Fantastic Monastic

The monks in every season
sing the world to its completion --- chanting psalms and hymns
to the glory of Christ Jesus.

How odd,
that so much would depend
on those who own
nothing more
than a robe
and a pair of sandals!

And in this congregation of burning
human candles,
the works of devotion
are as steady as the sun
in its ritual motions
of eternal returning.

With incense thickly burning,
the daily prayers revolving,
bread and wine dissolving
into this:

the body of our most sacred days.

As evening descends,
monastic crickets sing their songs
of praise...

it is a liturgy without end.

St. Francis speaks with birds--
and the cloistered worm
beneath the earth
softly says:

Amen.
