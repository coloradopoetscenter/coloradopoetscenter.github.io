Amy Wray Irish

Amy Wray Irish has an MFA in creative writing from the University of Notre Dame.  Her chapbook of poems and art, Creation Stories(Green Fuse Press 2008), is available in a free-to-view eBook format at http://www.poetscoop.org/free.htm#CreationStories. 

Her work can also be seen in 100 Words, Apocalypse, Ariel, The Bend (formerly Dánta), Improv (coming in 2009), Neologisms, the Notre Dame Review, Poetry Motel, Poetry Saves (coming in 2009), River King Poetry Supplement, and Wazee.  She was featured poet in Wazee’s Spring 2003 edition.

Originally from the Chicago area, Amy now lives in Littleton with her husband, also a writer, and their son. For more information or to purchase a copy of Creation Stories:
