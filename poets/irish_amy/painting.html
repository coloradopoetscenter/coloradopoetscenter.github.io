Painting In the Key of Sea 

"Blue and Green Music" -- Georgia O'Keeffe

Underwater electric, seductively
Simple, a kinetic message
Singing down the spine –

Pulsing below the surface, precariously
Close, blue-green currents
On canvas, beneath skin --

Ebbing intensity, insistently
Turquoise, sound swelling
Quiet rhythms past their river banks --

Ocean-colored currents, relentlessly
Relaxed, tangled and intertwined
Brushstrokes of sound –

Saxophone swirling tides, deliciously
Liquid, take a taste
Of jade-voiced water, wade in –

Don’t worry. I’ll teach you how to swim.

 

Forthcoming in Improv, 2009
