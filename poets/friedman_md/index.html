Biography

M. D. Friedman is a poet, a teacher, a musician and a photographer. His poems have appeared in Wired Art from Wired Hearts, Kookamonga Square, Job's Turkey, Arcade, The Green Horse and The Dry Creek Review.His fourth book of poetry, Where We Reach, combines his poetry with his original photographs and artwork. He is the founder of the Internet Poets' Cooperative website which features over 20 free volumes of e-books form poets from around the world. Also featured there are around 200 free audio recordings of Colorado poets reading their own work at the ever popular Poets' Co-op Open Readings.
