What I Mean by History

(for Malidoma Some)

 

We are taking a journey together.

This is not just a class

And what I'm trying to give you

Is not just information.

We are spending time together.

To make it meaningful

Means we're both engaged.

When I tell you, you will have

A shallow sense of life and

A shallow identity if you

have no history,

I don't say this to make you feel bad.

I say this to intrigue you,

To whet your appetite

For the inner journey.

The journey to inner strength

Where you will have

The soul fire to weather

The winds of trouble,

The pain that touches

Every life.

Without connection to the living tree

We call the ancestors

Those winds will blow you over,

Knock you down,

Beat you to the ground.

I know!

I've struggled up many times.

Been knocked down again

Over and over.

Without that ancestral voice

Saying get up! You're not unique.

We were knocked down.

We got up so you could Be!

Without that voice

Living inside you

We are lost

And the game is up!

That's what I mean

by history!
