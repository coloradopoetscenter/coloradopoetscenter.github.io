Biography

Sheryl Luna’s collection PITY THE DROWNED HORSES received the Andrés Montoya Prize and was published by the University of Notre Dame Press in 2005.

She was a finalist for the 2003 National poetry series and her work has appeared in Georgia Review, Prairie Schooner, Poetry Northwest, American Literary Review and elsewhere. She coordinates the learning center at the Community College of Denver’s Southwest campus and resides in Denver.
