Grass Shadows Cast on Snow

If stems and seed heads are beautiful,

so too their shadows quivering in the cold,

sketches drawn on the white unmarked morning.

Which will I be?

Grass bending in the wind

or shadows coming, going,

dependent on changing clouds?

 

Laurie Wagner Buyer

March 2, 2002
